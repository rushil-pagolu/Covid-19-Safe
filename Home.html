Home Remedies

What You Can do at Home

- Drink lots of water and other non-alcoholic fluids
- Over-the-counter medicines such as paracetamol and 
ibuprofen can help treat symptoms of fevers and headaches
- Avoid tasks that require a lot of energy and take rest at home
- Cough medicine can help reduce coughing and the spread of the virus
- Gargling a teaspoon of honey can help reduce a sore throat

What Not to do

- Do not use “natural cures” or herbal remedies instead of medicine
- Do not take unprescribed antibiotics or use antibacterial sanitizer and soap, 
due to them having no effect on a virus
